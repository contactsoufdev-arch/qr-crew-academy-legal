# Privacy Policy — QR Crew Academy

**Effective Date:** April 3, 2026  
**Last Updated:** April 3, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **QR Crew Academy**, a mobile training application designed to help Qatar Airways cabin crew applicants prepare for their selection process. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

By using QR Crew Academy, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account or use the app:

- **Email address** — used for account creation and login
- **Display name** — shown on your profile within the app
- **Quiz scores** — recorded to track your performance and progress
- **Module completion data** — stored to show your learning progress

We do **not** collect:
- Location data
- Device contacts or photos
- Payment information
- Any data for advertising purposes

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

- Create and manage your account
- Save and display your quiz scores and learning progress
- Provide a personalized training experience within the app

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

QR Crew Academy uses the following third-party services to operate:

### Supabase
We use [Supabase](https://supabase.com) as our backend database and authentication provider. Your account data and progress are stored securely on Supabase servers. Supabase is GDPR-compliant and does not use your data for advertising. See their [Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

### Google OAuth
You may optionally sign in using your Google account via Google OAuth. When you do, Google shares your email address and display name with us. We do not receive your Google password. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## 5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active.

## 6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Supabase enforces row-level security to ensure users can only access their own data.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

QR Crew Academy is intended for adults (18+) applying to cabin crew positions. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has submitted data through our app, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
